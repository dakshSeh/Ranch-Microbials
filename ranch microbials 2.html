Our Microbial Solutions

Revolutionizing Agriculture Through Microbial Innovation

At Ranch Microbials, we are dedicated to revolutionizing industrial microbe applications for sustainable agriculture, kitchen gardens, and lawns. Our advanced microbial solutions enrich soil health, enhance nutrient absorption, and promote plant resilience, leading to higher yields and healthier crops. Whether you're a large-scale farmer or a home gardening enthusiast, our eco-friendly microbes work naturally to boost growth, improve soil fertility, and reduce dependency on chemical fertilizers. With a commitment to sustainability and innovation, we strive to create a happier, more productive farming experience—because better soil means better harvests and a greener planet!

Nitrogen Fixing Bacteria

Azotobacter

Free-living nitrogen-fixing bacteria that enhance soil fertility and plant growth.

  • Improves nitrogen content in soil
  • Promotes plant growth
  • Enhances seed germination

Azospirillum

Associates with plant roots to fix atmospheric nitrogen and promote growth.

  • Fixes atmospheric nitrogen
  • Produces growth hormones
  • Improves root development

  • Enhances crop yield
